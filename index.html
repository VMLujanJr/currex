-About-

In early 2022, three young developers found the need to create an on-demand currency converter. The need arose from the developers having family overseas in different places and wanting to be able to send and recieve money to and from their family that are scattered all around the globe. Currex quickly became the answer to their problems. In a few short seconds, you are able to quickly convert most currencys into whichever currencys you need while delivering graphs displaying on demand exchange rates.
